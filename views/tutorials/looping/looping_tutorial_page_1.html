Why

There are times when you will need to repeat certain parts of your code. Imagine you are doing something really, really tedious, like
counting the number of spaghetti noodles on your plate. For a person to do that task it would take forever (not to mention it would
probably be quite messy to). It would be really easy to get tired and lose your count, forcing you to start over.

However, for computers that kind of counting is super easy. They never get tired or lose their place after all.

What

Loops are made up of two main parts. The Head and the Body.

  • The Head of a loop is also known as a conditional. This is because the head contains
    the conditions for the loop to continue.
  • The Body of a loop contains some amount of code that will be rerun every time the loop is repeated.

How

To use a loop in your with your block-based code you will generally follow these steps:

  1. Click on the Looping Menu to the left of thet workspace
  2. Choose the type of loop you want to use.
    More information about the types of loops will be in the next few sections.
  3. Edit the head of the loop so that it will loop in the way you want it to.
  4. Add the commands you want to be repeated each cycle of the loop to the body of the loop.

Be careful, it is possible to make loops that will never stop! These are known as "infinite loops".
