Why, What, and How.

Hello World is one of the earliest programs of almost all programmers.

It provides a very simple and easy method of introducing the most basic concepts of programming.

All it is is telling the computer to say "Hello World!" using a 'print' command.

What you do is put the "Hello World!" sentence into the print command, and run the code.
